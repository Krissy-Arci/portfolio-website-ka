Head to Toe

An online store where shoppers can find all of their hygiene essentials

  • Objective:

    An online store to help everyone find products quickly and easily to match their particular needs

  • Duration:

    2 Weeks

My Process

Research

I looked at online stores that offered similar products and subscription services like Ipsy, Ulta, Amazon, Hello Fresh and Birch Box, keeping note of areas where the design felt clean and simple and where there was clutter and potential difficulty for users to streamline their purchases and returns.

Ideation

Referring to the user stories provided in the project brief, it was my job to determine the key functionalities of the online store and design the user flow to include these functionalities.







Wireframes

The flow was then translated to low and mid-fidelity mobile wireframes so that the initial design could be used as a prototype for user testing.



I reached out to 5 potential Head to Toe users to test the flow of the mid-fidelity prototype. I chose one key flow, based on the user stories, and explained a scenario in which the they would be using the app. I asked them to login to their account and return an item.

Design

After synthesizing the user test results, I implemented the user feedback, edits and established style guide to high-fidelity screens.

Conclusion

  • What I enjoyed most:

    Streamlining all of the UI skill I’ve gained throughout my CareerFoundry UI Design course.

  • What I struggled with:

    Animating certain features like the accordion menu on the Item Description screen, but overcame the challenged with a tutorial and some trial and error.

  • Key Insights:

    Make sure the design is accessible- usability is the point! The first version of the icon buttons on the Landing screen didn’t provide enough contrast for readability, but I used Webaim’s contrast checker to ensure the text is legible.
