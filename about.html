About

My Story

I’m an Artist, Teacher and Childcare Professional turned User Interface Designer in Philadelphia, PA. After years of juggling teaching and childcare while trying to maintain some creative practice, I did some soul-searching to figure out what I really wanted from my life and career. I found that UI Design was the perfect way for me to combine the things I care about-creativity, independence, and most importantly, helping people! So I bought a new laptop and enrolled in a CareerFoundry bootcamp. With my user design skills, frontend development capabilities and creative background, my longterm goal is to grow within a team of like-minded, ambitious and passionate people - and always keep learning!

I live with my husband and our two hilariously mischievous cats. When I’m not in my home office/ artist studio, you can probably find me in my tiny kitchen, listening to jazz and preparing an unnecessarily elaborate snack.

Want to know more about me? Feel free to check out my resume and reach out - I'd love to connect!
