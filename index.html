Head to Toe

Head to Toe is an online shop where users can find all of their favorite hygiene essentials, and even curate their own box for recurring shipments so they never forget an item at the store again. This project and its responsive design relies on user stories and testing to determine its key features.

Wag'n

Wag’n is a native mobile app for iPhone and Android providing ride share services for pets and pet parents, so they can get to their vet or pet hospital quickly, safely and comfortably. The design focuses on Human Interface and Material Design guidelines to ensure usability and familiarity across both mobile platforms.
